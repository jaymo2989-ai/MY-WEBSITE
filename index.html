About The School

Muhoho High School is a renowned Extra-County public boys' secondary school located in Gatundu South Constituency, Kiambu County.

Established in the 1960s, the school has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, discipline, leadership, and technical education.

The school motto is: "To serve is to reign."

Academic Performance

KCSE Excellence

The school consistently posts strong KCSE results with many students qualifying for university admission.

2023 Results

The Class of 2023 recorded excellent performance and was recognized during the 2024 Education Day celebrations.

Mean Score Trend

Muhoho High School maintains a strong KCSE mean score ranging between B- and B+.
